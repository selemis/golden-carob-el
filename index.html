Welcome

Welcome to the website of GOLDEN CAROB P.C..

GOLDEN CAROB was founded in July of 2013. The company's goal is to produce, trade and process the fruit of the carob tree.

GOLDEN CAROB entered the market dynamically, owning mechanical equipment that can cope with plant processing capacity of 20,000 tons per year, our company aims from the beginning, to become the market leader.

Our company is surrounded by experienced executives in the domain (services, research, production and distribution) and reputable partners in places of production and aims to bring out a greek product with endless applications.

Our goal is to promote the cultivation and to give the appropriate added value by investing in all phases of production of products and raw materials.

Wide range of carob products have application in the fields of human health food, pharmaceutical, cosmetics, beverage, livestock and poultry feed, tanning and dyeing and a plethora of others.

In our website you can find detailed information about our products, our manufacturing facilities, the operation and organization of our company, its prospects and its objectives.

We are always willing to respond to every contact and to discuss and inform you about the carob tree and its fruits, from their planting to their final process phase.
